The Importance of Elephants

SAVANNA ELEPHANTS (Loxodonta africana) are the largest living land mammals. They are intelligent, have close family bonds, and complex social networks. As with all other species, elephants play crucial roles within their ecosystem, with numerous species of plant and animal being affected by their presence â€“ or absence. They are adaptable animals that can live in a wide variety of habitats from deserts to open savanna, wetlands, woodlands, and forests.

What is a laptop?

A laptop, sometimes called a notebook computer by manufacturers, is a battery- or AC-powered personal computer (PC) smaller than a briefcase. A laptop can be easily transported and used in temporary spaces such as on airplanes, in libraries, temporary offices and at meetings. A laptop can be turned into a desktop computer with a docking station, which is a hardware frame that supplies connections for peripheral input/output devices such as a monitor, keyboard and printer
